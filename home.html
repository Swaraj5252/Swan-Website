About Swan Safety Services

The organizations are bringing in the best possible technologies, in both, the industry and service sector to serve World class product & services. The best possible technologies will certainly need the back up of safe work environment, strong management systems, trained manpower, and knowledge up-gradation. Swan Safety Services is Safety & Occupational Health Consulting Organisation. It was incorporated in October 2014. It is a proprietary firm established by Mr. Anand Keshav Gadre. We provide services in the area of Occupational Health & Safety for various types of organisations ranging from factories to Service Sectors. We also conduct Community Trainings.

Brief History

After gaining Hands on Experience in Process / Production & Safety, Anand Keshav Gadre decided to embark on career of Safety & Health Consultant. Thus Swan Safety Services was conceptualised in April 2013 & established in October 2014. During last 6 years Swan Safety Services has served entire gamut of industries / a variety of industries viz. Engineering, Electrical Components, Automobile Components, Power Plants, Chemicals, Petroleum, Plastics, Food, Pharmaceuticals, Personal Care, Warehouse

Profile of Proprietor

Mr. ANAND KESHAV GADRE is a Safety professional having 27 years of Work Experience (Operation - Process & Safety) including industrial work experience and consulting. He has received his Diploma in Chemical Engineering and Advance Diploma in Industrial Safety from Maharashtra State Board of Tech Education. His industrial work experience includes working with leading industries like Reliance Industries Ltd Patalganga Petrochemical Complex (MH), Hikal Ltd Taloja (MH), & Gargi Huttenes Albertus Pvt Ltd. Khopoli (MH). Recognition: Mr. Anand Keshav Gadre has received recognition as a Safety Auditor from Directorate of Industrial Safety & Health (DISH) Maharashtra State.
